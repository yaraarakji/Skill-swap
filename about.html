About Skill Swap

Skill Swap is a student-built platform that helps LAU students exchange skills, time, and support without using money.

The problem we saw on campus

Many students struggle with assignments, presentations, and software, while other students already have those skills. However, there is no central place that connects them in a structured and fair way.

As a result, potential is wasted: students either pay for private tutoring, give up, or never discover that someone just one building away could have helped them.

Our solution

Skill Swap turns skills into a shared resource. Students offer what they know, and receive what they need in return. Time is the currency: one hour of helping someone earns one hour of learning from someone else.

The platform is designed to be simple, free, and campus-focused – tailored to LAU’s reality and course load.

Our mission

To build a culture of collaboration on campus where students feel comfortable asking for help and proud to share their skills.

  • Make learning support more accessible.
  • Encourage peer-to-peer teaching.
  • Highlight the value of student skills.

Our vision

In the short term, Skill Swap aims to become LAU’s go-to platform for academic and practical support between students.

In the long term, the model can scale to other universities in Lebanon, forming a wider network of student-led knowledge exchange.
