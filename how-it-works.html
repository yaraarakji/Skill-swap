How Skill Swap Works

3 simple steps → Offer. Match. Learn.

1. Offer a Skill

Students list one or more skills they can teach — languages, software, presentations, writing, anything.

2. Get Matched

Your offer appears on the Skill Board. Other students contact you if they need that skill.

3. Swap Time

You teach for one hour → you earn one learning hour. Time is the currency.
