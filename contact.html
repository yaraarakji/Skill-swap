How this page would work in a full version

In this prototype, the contact form is not connected to an email server or database. Instead, it demonstrates where communication would happen in a real deployment.

  • Messages could be sent to a dedicated LAU email address for the project team.
  • Feedback from students could be stored in a simple Google Sheet for analysis.
  • Supervisors or faculty could receive suggestions from students about improving the platform.

For MGT401, this page shows how communication would be integrated without requiring a complex back-end at the prototype stage.
