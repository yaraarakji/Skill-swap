Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to everything you need to know about Skill Swap.

What is Skill Swap?

It’s a student-to-student platform where you exchange skills instead of paying money.

Who can join?

Any LAU student from any major can participate.

Is it free?

Yes! Skill Swap is completely free. You exchange time, not money.

Do I need to be an expert to teach?

No. You just need to know something better than someone else — even basics help.

How do I contact another student?

Use the Instagram or email they include when submitting their skill.

Can I offer or learn more than one skill?

Yes! You can offer multiple skills and use your time credits to learn different ones.
