How Skill Swap works in 3 steps

Simple, transparent, and designed for busy students.

1. Offer a skill

List something you can help with: a course you passed, a software you use, or a language you speak.

2. Appear on the Skill Board

Your offer shows on the Skill Board so other students can reach out via Instagram or email.

3. Swap time

For every hour you give, you earn one hour to learn from someone else.
