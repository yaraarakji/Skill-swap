Skill Swap is a peer-to-peer skill exchange platform for LAU students. You teach for one hour, you earn one hour to learn from someone else.

Join the pilot → How it works

What is Skill Swap?

A campus-based platform where students can offer skills they already have (languages, software, presentations, etc.) and receive help in areas they want to improve — without paying money.

This is the prototype version, created as part of the MGT401 course at LAU.

Why Skill Swap?

We saw students struggling with assignments, software, and presentations — while other students had exactly those skills. Skill Swap turns that gap into collaboration.

Free learning

No tutoring fees. Your time is the currency.

Student to student

Learn from someone who already took your course or uses the tool daily.

LAU-focused

Built for our campus, our courses, and our reality.
