About the project

This prototype was developed by LAU students (MGT401) to demonstrate how peer learning can support SDG 4, 8, and 10. The platform connects students who can teach with students who want to learn.

How Skill Swap works

Three simple steps to start learning and teaching.

1. Sign up

Tell us what you can teach and what you want to learn.

2. Get matched

We pair you with someone who needs your skill.

3. Swap & earn

Teach for 1 hour → earn 1 hour to spend on another skill.
