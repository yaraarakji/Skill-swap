Skill Board (Prototype)

This page shows how Skill Swap could display skills offered by LAU students. For the MGT401 project, this is a front-end prototype – in a full version it would be connected to a real database or Google Sheet.

Prototype view – search and filters are not active yet. They illustrate the future design.

Sample skills “offered” in the prototype

These example cards show how students’ offers could appear on the Skill Board. Names, faculties, and skills are placeholders for the presentation.

French Conversation (B2)

Languages

Offered by: LAU student – Arts & Sciences

1-to-1 French practice for oral exams, presentations, and everyday conversation.

Wants to learn: Excel basics or Canva for presentations

Format: On campus or online · 1h sessions

Excel & Basic Data Analysis

Software

Offered by: Business student

Help with formulas, tables, and organizing data for projects and reports.

Wants to learn: Academic writing or SPSS intro

Format: Library study rooms · 1–2h swaps

Presentation Design & Public Speaking

Presentations

Offered by: Interior Architecture student

Support with structuring slides, visual design, and practicing presentations.

Wants to learn: Photoshop basics or AutoCAD tips

Format: In person · small groups or 1-to-1

SPSS Intro for Psych & Education

Software

Offered by: Psychology student

Step-by-step support with entering data, running basic tests, and reading outputs.

Wants to learn: Graphic design or Italian basics

Format: Online (Teams/Zoom) · screen sharing

Study Planning & Time Management

Study Support

Offered by: Student mentor

Help with building weekly study plans, breaking down deadlines, and avoiding last-minute stress.

Wants to learn: Advanced PowerPoint or AutoCAD

Format: On campus · 1h planning sessions

Canva & Visual Posters for Clubs

Presentations

Offered by: Design-oriented student

Creating social media posts, event posters, and simple branding kits using free tools.

Wants to learn: Statistics or academic reading strategies

Format: Flexible · online or on campus

In the full Skill Swap system, each card would be automatically created from the Join form and stored in a shared database or Google Sheet for all LAU students to browse.
